Aliign

If you add the class 'flex-center" you will be able to align the content in the middle regardless of size. You can also use "flex-end" or "flex-start" to align it at the sart or end of the div.

Fill

Usually the 'Divs' inside the flex content have a property of flex-grow:1, tha enables them to be divided equally inside the container. you can, however, make a div bigger by the use of "flex_g[X]" ([X] should be replaced by a number between 2 to 5)
on a smaller screen the difference between divs will be reduced
